# Product Team Demo Data

Launch-readiness review:

- Product managers inspect one representative answer before launch.
- A good answer must include citations and a trace ID so the team can explain why it was generated.
- Evaluation metrics such as pairwise accuracy, NDCG, recall, and coverage help compare retrieval behavior across profile changes.
- Optimization candidates help choose a launch profile without relying on a single manual answer review.

Decision rule:

Launch only when the representative answer is grounded, the trace can be inspected, evaluation metrics meet the product bar, and optimization does not introduce unacceptable latency or quality regressions.
